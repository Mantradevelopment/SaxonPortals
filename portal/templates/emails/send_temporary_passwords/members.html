If you have received your login details but you are experiencing issues, please note the following:

  1. ur portal is most compatible with Google Chrome and Firefox
  2. Before logging in, ensure that your pop up blocker is turned off
  3. Our portal is not currently compatible with iPhone

In case you are facing any issues logging in to the portal with the temporary password provided. Please ensure that:

  1. You are copying the right password (without any extra spaces). This is the most cases where issues occur.

If you have made too many attempts to access your account, you may reset your password by completing the following steps:

  1. Select "Forgot your password"
  2. Select "By Using Email"
  3. Enter your Silver Thatch Pensions member number and email address provided on your member enrollment form
  4. A temporary password will be emailed. Once you're able to log in, you'll be prompted to enter a new password, security question and answer. Keep this information as safe as you would your online banking details.
